About FuzzMe

FuzzMe is an app designed to mask your online presence. With FuzzMe, we protect your privacy by flooding the internet with either images, or text prompts which spread false information about you. By flooding the internet with enough false information, it will become impossible to distinguish fact from reality. Your name can no longer be tainted online since we will taint it first. Read about any of our features below.

Mask Me

With MaskMe, we take any image that you have of yourself and spread duplicates around the internet with different faces on the picture. This way, you'll have deniability if someone claims that you are present in an image. Simply upload an image you want to get masked and brush over your face with our transparent brush. Then, click upload and we'll show you the images we'll spread around.

Fuzz Me

With FuzzMe, we take your face and flood the internet with pictures of you doing a host of activities. This way no one will know what you are actually doing.
